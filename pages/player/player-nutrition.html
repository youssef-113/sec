Nutrition Tips

Personalized recommendations from your nutritionist

Stay Hydrated

Drink water consistently throughout the day. Aim for 500ml 2 hours before training and 150-250ml every 15-20 minutes during exercise.

Meal Timing

Eat your pre-training meal 3-4 hours before exercise. Have a light snack 30-60 minutes before training for optimal energy levels.

Post-Training Recovery

Consume protein and carbohydrates within 30 minutes after training to optimize muscle recovery and glycogen replenishment.

Evening Nutrition

Avoid heavy meals 3 hours before bedtime. A light protein snack like Greek yogurt can support overnight muscle recovery.
